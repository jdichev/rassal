A slide with a simple layout

Kamon are celebrated for their minimalist, monochromatic, and circular designs. Most symbols are derived from nature or geometry, with the most common motifs being:

  • Plants: Paulownia, Wisteria, and the Chrysanthemum (the Imperial seal).
  • Animals: Cranes, butterflies, and hawks.
  • Objects: Fans, arrows, and stylized Chinese characters
